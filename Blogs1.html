Healthy Lifestyle and Progress

What is it?

With healthier lifestyle , i would point towards the thought of having a ballance between everything like work,good habitsand daily deeds.The topic seems to be simple but still it seems many people in the corporate world are struggling in this aspect. This i am saying because i had a self observation regarding this.Also we may say progress at some time is a reflection of healthy lifestyle'

Struggles and genuine reasons

Some people have genuine reasons regarding not opting for good habits like rising early as there may be varying shifts work in the company they work.Some people have real struggle in building their career.The reasons may be genuine but not everlasting, so try your level best to lead a heathier life.In my view one or two habits may be wrong ,if you all the time being lazy won't perform heathier growth related tasks then your success may be hindered by this.

What to do?

In my views, if somebody has got his or her health affected or any ill effects he/she should slowly opt for good habits like rising early, eating fruitful,gyming,sports or any other task producing positive heath effects.You may not do these deeds all the time but you may definitely devote some time per your schedule. Adding a sport or gyming also may lead a fight with depression thereby, releasing good hormones. My suggestion for one who sit all the time and work is try a month with physical activites one hour atleast and you will feel the difference.At last i would conclude ,that progress is in direct proportion with heathy lifestyle.I wish the one reading this blog may lead a heathier life.

In my views, HEALTHY LIFESTYLE = PROGRESS = EXTINCTION OF STRUGGLES
